The Piano Academy at Fort Lewis College-Summer 2025!

We would love to play with you!

The Piano Academy at Fort Lewis College provides an exceptional opportunity for young intermediate to advanced pianists grades 4-12 to improve their skills and teamwork. Auditions are required. Daily private lessons and group coachings will improve sight-reading, technique, working with others, all while having fun. Students perform a final recital for family and friends on Thursday, and can experience learning piano literature, music history, and music theory in a collegiate classroom. They will also get to participate in physical and creative activities while enjoying the breathtaking views on Fort Lewis Collegeâ€™s campus.


Audition videos and registration are due to the Piano Academy are due by Friday, March 14. Please perform one short piece with a camera angle that ensures that hands and feet are visible and provide a link to an unlisted video on YouTube. You can find the application on the 4CTMA website. Applicants may substitute their virtual audition by performing a work by auditioning at the FLC Piano and Strings Day (March 8th) or performing at one of the 2025 Durango Bach Festival Student Recitals (March 9th). Limited need-based scholarships available.


For more information, please contact Dr. Holly Quist at hrquist@fortlewis.edu
